Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2001, Stackly Consulting Group emerged from a simple belief: that businesses deserve more than generic advice. They deserve transformative partnerships that deliver measurable, lasting results.

What began as a boutique strategy firm with three consultants has grown into a globally recognized advisory powerhouse with offices in New York, London, Singapore, and Dubai. Our journey has been defined by the trust of over 150 enterprise clients and a relentless commitment to excellence.

Today, Stackly stands at the intersection of tradition and innovation — blending time-tested strategic frameworks with cutting-edge digital capabilities to solve the most complex business challenges of our time.
