FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical engagement last? +

Engagement durations vary based on scope and complexity. Strategic advisory engagements typically run 3–6 months, while full transformation programs can span 12–24 months. We tailor every engagement to your specific needs.

What industries do you specialize in? +

We have deep expertise across financial services, healthcare, technology, manufacturing, energy, consumer retail, professional services, and life sciences. Our consultants bring both industry-specific knowledge and cross-sector best practices.

How do you measure success? +

We establish clear KPIs and milestones at the outset of every engagement. Our approach is outcomes-focused — we measure success through tangible business results such as revenue growth, cost reduction, operational efficiency, and market share expansion.

Do you offer remote consulting services? +

Yes, we offer flexible engagement models including fully remote, hybrid, and on-site consulting. Our global team leverages advanced collaboration tools to deliver seamless service regardless of location.

What is your client onboarding process? +

Our onboarding process begins with a comprehensive discovery phase where we assess your business, objectives, and challenges. We then develop a tailored engagement plan, align on deliverables and timelines, and deploy the appropriate team.
